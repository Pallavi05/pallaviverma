Call and Response

2024Performer

An improvised collaborative performance that blends music and dance, examining our physical reactions to soundscapes present in both natural and urban environments. It investigates the interplay between dancers and musicians, contemplating the comfort we derive from birdsong contrasted with the discomfort of city noise.

Performed at

  • Max Muller Bhawan, Delhi Nov 2024
